A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, also known as A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, was an Indian scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
